# Strategic Focus

Short-Term Focus Areas:
-----------------------
1. Improve ball security in high-pressure situations.
2. Develop 2–3 secondary scoring options beyond Player X.
3. Enhance defensive set plays against fast-break transitions.

Long-Term Focus Areas:
----------------------
1. Conditioning for late-game stamina to sustain SH% and reduce TO.
2. Structured set plays to diversify offense.
3. Training drills for ground ball recoveries and draw controls.

Measurable Targets:
-------------------
- Reduce turnovers by 20% in final quarter.
- Lift team SH% from 18% → 22% season average.
- Achieve positive ground ball differential in 75% of games.
